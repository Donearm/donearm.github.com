About Me

I was a sysadmin before a developer. Today I would have started as DevOps Engineer. I built and rebuilt hundreds of computers, for learning, and tried an insane amount of different OSes (Linux has always been the favourite one). Then I fell in love with Python and started my coding journey. Until I turned to my original, childhood’s passion, writing, and merged knowledge with passion in becoming a technical writer. Nowadays I mostly write, documentation, technical articles, some copywriting and aim to continue to do so for the foreseeable future.

Computer-related stuff aside, I enjoy reading, primarily fantasy and historical books, inventing new recipes, mixing drinks, reviewing coffee and being interested in anything that’s art. I cannot live without music and a trip to the beach in the summer. Nor without my laptop.
